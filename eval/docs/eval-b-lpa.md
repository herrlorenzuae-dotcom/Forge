LIMITED PARTNERSHIP AGREEMENT (EXCERPTS)
Aurora Catalyst Partners Fund, L.P.

RECITALS
WHEREAS, the parties wish to set forth their respective rights and obligations with respect to the affairs of the Partnership; and WHEREAS, the General Partner has been duly organized under the laws of Delaware.

Section 3.2 — Drawdown Notices
Each drawdown notice shall be delivered to the Limited Partners no fewer than ten (10) Business Days prior to the due date for the related capital contribution, and shall state the purpose of the drawdown in reasonable detail.

Section 4.4 — Principal Events
Upon the death or disability of any Principal, the General Partner shall give written notice to the Limited Partners within five (5) Business Days of becoming aware of such event.

Section 5.7 — Concentration Limit
The General Partner shall not cause the Partnership to invest more than 25% of aggregate Commitments in any single Portfolio Company without the prior written consent of the Advisory Committee.

Section 7.3 — Quarterly Statements
The General Partner shall furnish unaudited financial statements to each Limited Partner within sixty (60) days after the end of each of the first three fiscal quarters.

Section 8.2 — Sustainability Reporting
The General Partner shall publish an annual sustainability report and deliver it to the Limited Partners within ninety (90) days after each fiscal year end.

Section 9.1 — Pledges
No Limited Partner may pledge its interest in the Partnership without the prior written consent of the General Partner.
