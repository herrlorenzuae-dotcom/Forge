SIDE LETTER — MERIDIAN BAY INSURANCE COMPANY
Halcyon Industrial Fund II, L.P.

Paragraph 1 — Gaming Sector Investments
The General Partner shall notify Meridian Bay Insurance Company in writing no fewer than twelve (12) Business Days prior to the closing of any Portfolio Investment in the gaming sector, and shall deliver a written risk memorandum describing such investment no later than thirty (30) days after such closing.

Paragraph 2 — Valuation Policy
The General Partner shall not amend the Fund's valuation policy in a manner adverse to Meridian Bay Insurance Company without the prior written consent of Meridian Bay Insurance Company.

Paragraph 3 — Acknowledgment
Meridian Bay Insurance Company acknowledges that it has received and reviewed the Fund's privacy notice and the most recent annual report, and that nothing in this Paragraph creates any obligation on the part of the General Partner.

Paragraph 4 — Regulatory Data
The General Partner shall deliver to Meridian Bay Insurance Company a statement of Solvency II look-through data within forty (40) days after the end of each fiscal quarter.

Paragraph 5 — Excused Sectors
Meridian Bay Insurance Company shall be excused from participation in any Portfolio Investment in tobacco or gambling enterprises to the extent such participation would violate its written investment policy.
